April showers by Polder

Today I would like to introduce April showers, a kidswear collection created by a French brand that we absolutely love. Named Polder, it is a Parisian brand created by two Dutch sisters, Madelon Lanteri-Laura and Natalie Vodegel in 2000. Known for its fashion accessories such as handbags and jewellery, as well as for its collection of ready-to-wear, this brand has become a “must-have” in every girl’s closet.

 The brand uses a soft colour palette, mixing neutral colours with dusty tones. 

Since they begun, handmade work has had a great importance in all their collection, adding a handcrafted value.

Their shoes are also irresistible. We have a soft spot for the aqua blue clogs!

We love when little girls dress in black and even more if they have a bag to match
